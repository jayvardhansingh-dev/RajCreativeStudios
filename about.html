About Raj Creative Studios

History & Mission

Founded in 2004, Raj Creative Studios has been capturing life's most beautiful moments for over 22 years. We are passionate about turning special occasions into timeless memories through professional photography, cinematic videography, and creative storytelling.


From weddings and pre-weddings to birthdays, engagements, anniversaries, and corporate events, our team is dedicated to delivering high-quality visuals that reflect emotions, joy, and unforgettable experiences.


With modern equipment, creative editing techniques, drone coverage, and a customer-first approach, we have successfully served hundreds of happy clients across different cities and events.


At Raj Creative Studios, we don't just take photos — we capture stories, emotions, and memories that last forever.
